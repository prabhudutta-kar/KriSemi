About Us

We deliver landmark projects

Krisemi Design is specialized in ASIC design with mixed signal IP, firmware solutions for large telecommunication systems, photonic Integrated circuits and FPGA solutions.

KriSemi has successfully applied in house analog IP and firmware solutions to many complicated designes.The applications cover high performance computing (HPC), wireless communication, PC peripherals and high speed data transfer.

To become a leading edge chip design company in the world and provide more complete solution to customer chips, Krisemi is also committed to develop HPC solutions and mixed signal IP with lower nodes.
